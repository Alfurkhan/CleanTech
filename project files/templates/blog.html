About CleanTech

Revolutionizing waste management through artificial intelligence and transfer learning

Project Overview

CleanTech is an innovative AI-powered waste management solution that leverages the power of deep learning to classify waste into three primary categories: Biodegradable, Recyclable, and Trash.

Mission

To promote environmental sustainability by making waste classification accessible, accurate, and educational for individuals and organizations.

Vision

A world where AI-powered tools help reduce waste mismanagement and promote proper disposal practices globally.

AI Model Architecture

VGG16 Transfer Learning Model

Our waste classification model is built upon the VGG16 architecture, a proven convolutional neural network originally trained on ImageNet. We've adapted this powerful model specifically for waste classification tasks.

Model Architecture:
  • Base Model: VGG16 (ImageNet pre-trained)
  • Pooling: Global Average Pooling 2D
  • Dense Layer: 512 neurons with ReLU activation
  • Dropout: 0.5 rate for regularization
  • Output: 3 classes with Softmax activation

Waste Categories

Biodegradable

Organic waste that can decompose naturally and be composted.

Examples:
  • Fruit peels and cores
  • Vegetable scraps
  • Food leftovers
  • Garden waste
Recyclable

Materials that can be processed and reused to create new products.

Examples:
  • Glass bottles and jars
  • Plastic containers
  • Paper and cardboard
  • Metal cans and foil
Trash

Non-recyclable and non-biodegradable waste requiring proper disposal.

Examples:
  • Disposable utensils
  • Plastic bags
  • Medical waste
  • Batteries (special handling)
